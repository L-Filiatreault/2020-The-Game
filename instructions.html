Instructions on how to Play the Game

Use the arrow keys ↑↓ ← → on your keyboard to move the avatar up, down, left and right on the screen.

Collect all 10 toilet paper packs in Costco to win the game!

Avoid touching the Karens as they take a life each time.

With each toilet paper pack you collect, the Karens' speed increases.

You have 3 lives in total. When you reach 0 it's Game Over.

Keep an eye on your Stress Level, as your avatar will slow down as it increases. When it reaches 100% it's Game Over.

Drink the randomly-generated purple box of wine to reduce the Stress Level and boost your speed around the store.

To begin playing press on the 'Start' button.

If you wish to restart your game, press the 'Reset' button.


Player character: The avatar which you control while you play the game.

Karen: Rude, pushy and will kill your avatar while it prowls up and down the aisles for the toilet paper.

Toilet Paper: A precious commodity in these dire times, the sought-after prize of many.

Box of wine: This item will help calm down and improve your avatar's speed as it navigates through Costco.
